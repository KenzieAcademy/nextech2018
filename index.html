June 2018 Nextech Catapult

An eager-to-learn group of 23 rising high school Juniors and Seniors from Central Indiana who is part of Nextech Catapult program completed a 4 week coding session at Kenzie Academy. During this intensive course they learned Design thinking,the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and applied them to create several interactive games on their personal websites.
